Features of the School Portal

Grades & Progress

Keep track of your academic performance. View your grades in real-time, check assignment statuses, and see progress reports for each subject.

Timetable Management

Access your class schedule anytime. The timetable is updated in real-time with any changes made by your institution.

Assignments & Deadlines

View upcoming assignments, quizzes, and exams. Receive automatic reminders for due dates and submissions.

Study Materials

Access your course materials, syllabus, lecture notes, and other resources uploaded by instructors.

Communication Tools

Engage with teachers, students, and staff through direct messaging, group discussions, and announcements.

Notifications

Get real-time notifications for important updates such as grade postings, event announcements, and class changes.
